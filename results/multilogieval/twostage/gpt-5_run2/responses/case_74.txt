-- Conclusion to formalize: If James is not going to the party, did he plan his schedule well?
theorem goal : (¬ GoingToParty James) → PlanScheduleWell James := by
  -- This statement is not derivable from the given premises.
  -- From ¬GoingToParty James we can derive ¬FridayNight,
  -- then ¬WatchGame James, then ¬FinishHomework James,
  -- then ¬StudyEveryday James, and finally ¬PlanScheduleWell James.
  -- Hence the desired implication to PlanScheduleWell James cannot be proved.
  -- We therefore provide no derivation here.
  admit
