ANSWER: Failure

-- Attempt to prove Jane values wellness
theorem prove_jane_values_wellness : ValuesWellness Jane := by 
  -- T4 requires ExerciseRegularly Jane to infer ValuesWellness Jane
  -- But no axiom connects Energetic/EatsNutritiousFoods to ExerciseRegularly

  -- No logical path from T5 to ExerciseRegularly Jane
  admit  -- Proof fails

ANSWER: Failure

**Explanation:**
1. The critical error was misunderstanding implication direction in T4: It requires ValuesWellness to prove ExerciseRegularly, not vice versa.

   - Energetic to ExerciseRegularly
   - EatsNutritiousFoods to ValuesWellness

4. Thus, we cannot prove Jane values wellness from the premises.
