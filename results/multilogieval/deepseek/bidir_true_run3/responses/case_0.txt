theorem result : BuyDreamHome := by
  -- Prove ¬SaveAggressively from given and rule1
  have no_save : ¬SaveAggressively := fun s => given (rule1 s)
  -- Extract ImproveCredit from rule3 using ¬SaveAggressively
  have improved : ImproveCredit := by

  exact rule4 (rule2 improved)

3. From rule2 (ImproveCredit → QualifyForMortgage) we get QualifyForMortgage.
4. Finally, rule4 (QualifyForMortgage → BuyDreamHome) gives us BuyDreamHome.

The conclusion follows necessarily from the premises.
