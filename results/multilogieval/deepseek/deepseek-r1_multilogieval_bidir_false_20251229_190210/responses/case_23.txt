- Waking up early implies being energized (∀x, WokeUpEarly x → Energized x)
- Planning implies productivity (∀x, PlannedDay x → Productive x)
- There exists at least one person lacking energy or productivity (∃x, ¬Energized x ∨ ¬Productive x)

Given that Mark plans his day (PlannedDay Mark), we can infer he is productive (Productive Mark). However, there is no axiom connecting planning to self-discipline or waking up early. The existence of someone lacking energy/productivity doesn't constrain Mark specifically, as it could refer to another individual. Thus, we cannot prove that Mark lacks self-discipline (¬SelfDiscipline Mark) from the premises.
