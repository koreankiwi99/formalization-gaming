Using only Premises 1, 4, and 5:

2. Note `good_reviews → high_attendance`, so by contraposition, `¬high_attendance → ¬good_reviews`.
3. Thus, `¬high_attendance` implies `¬good_reviews`.

6. Since `¬big_star` and `big_star ∨ is_sequel`, it follows that `is_sequel`.
