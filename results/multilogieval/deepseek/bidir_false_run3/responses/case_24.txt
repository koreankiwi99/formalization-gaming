The goal is to prove the negation of the statement: "If there was no snow on the ground, then Mary loves winter." The negation is: "There was no snow on the ground and Mary does not love winter." After formalizing the context in Lean, we attempt to prove this negation. However, the premises do not provide information about Mary's actions or feelings in the scenario of no snow. Specifically:
- From no snow on the ground, we derive not freezing (using the contrapositive of the first axiom).
- This leads to not enough snow (from the disjunction).
- Consequently, the person S does not build a snowman, and thus S does not love winter (using the friend's rule).
- But there are no axioms linking Mary to the snow conditions or S's actions. Therefore, we cannot prove anything about Mary's love for winter when there is no snow.

Since the negation cannot be proven from the premises, the result is Failure.

axiom Mary : Person
axiom S : Person
axiom Freezing : Prop

axiom EnoughSnow : Prop
axiom BuildSnowman : Person → Prop

axiom A1 : Freezing → SnowOnGround
axiom A2 : BuildSnowman S → EnoughSnow
axiom A3 : Freezing ∨ ¬EnoughSnow
axiom A4 : ∀ p : Person, LovesWinter p → BuildSnowman p

-- Attempt to prove: ¬SnowOnGround ∧ ¬LovesWinter Mary
-- Assume ¬SnowOnGround
example : ¬ (¬SnowOnGround → LovesWinter Mary) := by
  unfold Not
  intro h
  -- We need to show ¬SnowOnGround ∧ ¬LovesWinter Mary
  -- Assume ¬SnowOnGround for the left part
  have no_snow : ¬SnowOnGround := by
    intro h1
    -- We will derive a contradiction later if needed, but we start with this assumption
    sorry
  -- Now we need ¬LovesWinter Mary
  -- From no_snow and A1, we get ¬Freezing
  have not_freezing : ¬Freezing := fun h_freeze => no_snow (A1 h_freeze)
  -- From A3 and not_freezing, we get ¬EnoughSnow
  have no_enough_snow : ¬EnoughSnow := by
    cases A3 with
    | inl freeze => contradiction
    | inr not_enough => exact not_enough
  -- From A2 (contrapositive) and no_enough_snow, we get ¬BuildSnowman S
  have no_build_S : ¬BuildSnowman S := fun h_build => no_enough_snow (A2 h_build)
  -- From A4 for S, we get ¬LovesWinter S
  have no_love_S : ¬LovesWinter S := fun h_love => no_build_S (A4 S h_love)
  -- However, we have no information about Mary: no axiom connects Mary to the snow conditions or S.
  -- We cannot prove ¬LovesWinter Mary.
  -- Therefore, we cannot complete the proof.
  sorry
