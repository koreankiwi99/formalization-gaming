ANSWER: Failure

**Explanation:**  
The goal was to prove the negation of "production costs increased" (i.e., `¬ProdCostIncrease last_quarter`). The proof attempt shows:
- If marketing occurred (`Marketing last_quarter`), we derive a contradiction because sales would have increased (via `A1` and `A2`), contradicting the premise `¬SalesIncrease last_quarter`.
- If production costs increased (`ProdCostIncrease last_quarter`), no contradiction arises because there are no axioms linking production cost increases to sales outcomes. The `sorry` in this branch admits the proof cannot be completed.  
Since the negation cannot be proven from the premises, the answer is **Failure**.
