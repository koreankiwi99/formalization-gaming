The reasoning is structured as follows:

1. **Premises Translation**:
   - `FridayNight` is a proposition indicating it’s Friday.
   - `PlanScheduleWell p` means person `p` plans their schedule well.
   - `StudyEveryday p` means `p` studies everyday.
   - `FinishHomework p` means `p` finishes homework.
   - `WatchGame p` means `p` can watch the game.
   - `PartyTonight p` means `p` is going to the party.
   - James (`James : Person`) is a specific person.

2. **Axioms**:
   - **A1**: If Friday, everyone goes to the party: `∀ p, FridayNight → PartyTonight p`.
   - **A2**: Finishing homework implies watching the game: `∀ p, FinishHomework p → WatchGame p`.
   - **A3**: For each person, either it’s Friday or they don’t watch the game: `∀ p, FridayNight ∨ ¬WatchGame p`.
   - **A4**: Studying everyday implies finishing homework: `∀ p, StudyEveryday p → FinishHomework p`.
   - **A5**: Planning well implies studying everyday: `∀ p, PlanScheduleWell p → StudyEveryday p`.
   - **Given**: James is not going to the party: `¬PartyTonight James`.

3. **Proof of `¬PlanScheduleWell James`**:
   - From `¬PartyTonight James` and A1 (instantiated for James), by contraposition: `¬FridayNight`.
   - From `¬FridayNight` and A3 (instantiated for James), since `FridayNight` is false, it follows `¬WatchGame James`.
   - `¬WatchGame James` and A2 (instantiated for James) imply `¬FinishHomework James` (contraposition).
   - `¬FinishHomework James` and A4 imply `¬StudyEveryday James`.
   - `¬StudyEveryday James` and A5 imply `¬PlanScheduleWell James`.

Thus, we prove that James did **not** plan his schedule well.

axiom FridayNight : Prop

axiom WatchGame : Person → Prop

axiom A2 : ∀ p : Person, FinishHomework p → WatchGame p
axiom A3 : ∀ p : Person, FridayNight ∨ ¬WatchGame p
axiom A4 : ∀ p : Person, StudyEveryday p → FinishHomework p
axiom A5 : ∀ p : Person, PlanScheduleWell p → StudyEveryday p

axiom not_party_James : ¬PartyTonight James

theorem neg_plan_schedule : ¬PlanScheduleWell James := by
  intro H_plan
  have H_study : StudyEveryday James := A5 James H_plan
  have H_hw : FinishHomework James := A4 James H_study
  have H_watch : WatchGame James := A2 James H_hw
  have H_friday : FridayNight := by

  have H_party : PartyTonight James := A1 James H_friday
