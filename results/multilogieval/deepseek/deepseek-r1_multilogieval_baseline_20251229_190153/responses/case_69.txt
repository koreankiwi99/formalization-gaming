The context states that for any athlete, if they train hard and remain focused, they will perform to the best of their ability. Given that Lebron remained focused but did not perform to the best of his ability, we can deduce that he did not train hard.

<lean>
axiom Athlete : Type
axiom Lebron : Athlete
axiom TrainsHard : Athlete → Prop
axiom RemainsFocused : Athlete → Prop
axiom PerformsBest : Athlete → Prop
axiom coaches_preach : ∀ a : Athlete, TrainsHard a → RemainsFocused a → PerformsBest a
axiom focused : RemainsFocused Lebron
axiom not_performed_best : ¬ PerformsBest Lebron

theorem Lebron_did_not_train_hard : ¬ TrainsHard Lebron := by
  intro train_hard
  have perf_best : PerformsBest Lebron := coaches_preach Lebron train_hard focused
  exact not_performed_best perf_best
</lean>

We proved that Lebron did not train hard. Therefore, the answer to "Did Lebron train hard?" is no.

ANSWER: No
