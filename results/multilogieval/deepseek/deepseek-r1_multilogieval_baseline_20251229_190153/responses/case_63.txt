The reasoning involves modus tollens on the implication provided in the context. The context states: if someone plants disease-resistant varieties, then proper watering and feeding lead to a bountiful harvest. Linda watered and fed properly but did not have a bountiful harvest. Therefore, Linda must not have planted disease-resistant varieties.

<lean>
axiom Person : Type
axiom Linda : Person
axiom PlantsDiseaseResistant : Person → Prop
axiom WaterAndFeedProperly : Person → Prop
axiom BountifulHarvest : Person → Prop

axiom gardener_saying : ∀ (p : Person), 
  PlantsDiseaseResistant p → (WaterAndFeedProperly p → BountifulHarvest p)

axiom watered_fed : WaterAndFeedProperly Linda
axiom no_harvest : ¬ BountifulHarvest Linda

theorem not_planted : ¬ PlantsDiseaseResistant Linda := by
  intro h
  have harvest : BountifulHarvest Linda := gardener_saying Linda h watered_fed
  exact no_harvest harvest

-- The question asks: "Did Linda plant disease-resistant varieties?"
-- We have proved she did NOT (¬PlantsDiseaseResistant Linda)
</lean>

ANSWER: No
