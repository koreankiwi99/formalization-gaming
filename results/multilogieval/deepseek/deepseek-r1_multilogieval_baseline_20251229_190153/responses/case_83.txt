The reasoning involves implications and disjunctions that connect sunny weather to the team holding their heads high. The key steps are:
1. Sunny weather implies children play (A2)
2. The disjunction (team practices ∨ ¬children play) becomes team practices when children play (since ¬Play is false)
3. Team practicing implies preparedness (A1)
4. Preparedness implies confidence (A4)
5. Confidence implies holding heads high (A5)

Thus, sunny weather leads to the team holding their heads high.

<lean>
axiom Practice : Prop
axiom Prepared : Prop
axiom Sunny : Prop
axiom Play : Prop
axiom Confidence : Prop
axiom HeadHigh : Prop

axiom A1 : Practice → Prepared
axiom A2 : Sunny → Play
axiom A3 : Practice ∨ ¬ Play
axiom A4 : Prepared → Confidence
axiom A5 : Confidence → HeadHigh

theorem sunny_implies_headhigh : Sunny → HeadHigh := by
  intro hSunny
  have hPlay : Play := A2 hSunny
  have hPractice : Practice := by
    cases A3 with
    | inl p => exact p
    | inr np => contradiction
  exact A5 (A4 (A1 hPractice))
</lean>
