1. He provided adequate sunlight and watering.
2. His tomatoes did not grow large and plentiful.

The goal is to prove the affirmative statement: "Farmer Joe planted the tomato seeds properly." In Lean, we model the scenario with axioms for the rule and the facts about Farmer Joe. However, from the rule and the facts, we can deduce that if Joe had planted properly, then his tomatoes would have grown. Since they did not grow, we can prove that he did **not** plant properly.
