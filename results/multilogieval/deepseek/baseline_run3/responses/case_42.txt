- Finally, by axiom `R4`, `PlayOutside` implies `SleepWell`.

axiom FluSeason : Prop

axiom R2 : FluSeason → GetSick
axiom R3 : Sunny ∨ ¬GetSick
axiom R4 : PlayOutside → SleepWell
