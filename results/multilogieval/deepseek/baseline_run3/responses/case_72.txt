  3. Either close to dinnertime or won't make dinner: `CloseToDinnertime ∨ ¬MakeDinner`
  4. If fridge empty, must go shopping: `FridgeEmpty → GoShopping`
  5. If pantry empty, fridge empty: `PantryEmpty → FridgeEmpty`

- **Question**: Does `StartCooking → PantryEmpty` hold?
