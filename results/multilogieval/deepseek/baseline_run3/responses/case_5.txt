axiom Understands : Person → Prop

theorem mary_seeks_advice : SeeksAdvice Mary := by
  have no_understands : ¬ Understands Mary := fun h => T1 (A2 Mary h)

2. From ¬U and A1, we get ¬L (if she didn't understand needs, she didn't learn botany)
