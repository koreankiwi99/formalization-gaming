- Goal: Prove `L Sarah` (Sarah listens attentively)

- The disjunction `R Sarah ∨ L Sarah` cannot be resolved to `L Sarah` without additional assumptions (e.g., that `¬P Sarah` implies `¬R Sarah`, which is unsupported).

**Conclusion:** The premises do not provide sufficient information to prove that Sarah listens attentively to music. The required logical connection between "not practicing daily" and "listening attentively" is missing.
