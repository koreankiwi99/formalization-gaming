The reasoning involves Lebron's training, focus, and performance. By assuming that Lebron trained hard and applying the coaching principle to his situation, a contradiction arises between his performance outcomes. Therefore, the assumption that he trained hard is false.

<lean>
axiom Athlete : Type
axiom Lebron : Athlete
axiom TrainsHard : Athlete → Prop
axiom RemainsFocused : Athlete → Prop
axiom PerformsBest : Athlete → Prop
axiom Preaching : ∀ a : Athlete, TrainsHard a → (RemainsFocused a → PerformsBest a)
axiom LebronFocused : RemainsFocused Lebron
axiom LebronNotPerformed : ¬ PerformsBest Lebron

theorem Lebron_no_train : ¬ TrainsHard Lebron := 
  fun assume_train : TrainsHard Lebron =>
  have contra : PerformsBest Lebron := 
    Preaching Lebron assume_train LebronFocused
  show False from LebronNotPerformed contra
</lean>
