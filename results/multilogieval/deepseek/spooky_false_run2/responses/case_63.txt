The gardener's common saying establishes a conditional relationship: if a gardener plants disease-resistant varieties and waters/feeds properly, they will have a bountiful harvest. Linda watered/feed properly but did not have a bountiful harvest. By contraposition, Linda must not have planted disease-resistant varieties.

axiom Gardener : Type
axiom Linda : Gardener
axiom PlantsDR : Gardener → Prop
axiom WaterFeedProperly : Gardener → Prop
axiom BountifulHarvest : Gardener → Prop
axiom common_saying : ∀ (p : Gardener), PlantsDR p → (WaterFeedProperly p → BountifulHarvest p)
axiom linda_water_feed : WaterFeedProperly Linda
axiom linda_not_bountiful : ¬ BountifulHarvest Linda

theorem linda_not_DR : ¬ PlantsDR Linda := by
  intro h  -- Assume Linda planted disease-resistant varieties
  have wf_implies_bh : WaterFeedProperly Linda → BountifulHarvest Linda := common_saying Linda h
  have bh : BountifulHarvest Linda := wf_implies_bh linda_water_feed
  exact linda_not_bountiful bh  -- Contradiction
