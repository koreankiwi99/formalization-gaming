The axioms are:
1. ∀ (p : Plate), IstanbulPlate p → beginsWith34 p

   (Plates that don't begin with 34 are not from Istanbul)

Proof:
From premise3, we know that Joe's plate is from Istanbul (IstanbulPlate joePlate).
Then, by premise1 applied to joePlate, we get: IstanbulPlate joePlate → beginsWith34 joePlate.
Since we have IstanbulPlate joePlate, we can directly conclude beginsWith34 joePlate.

Therefore, the proof is straightforward by applying premise1 to joePlate and using the fact that Joe's plate is from Istanbul.

We do not need the other premises (premise2, premise4, premise5) for this proof because they are about plates that are not from Istanbul or about Tom's plate, which is irrelevant to Joe's plate.
