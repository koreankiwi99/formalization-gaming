```lean

axiom Person : Type

-- Predicates
axiom isProfessionalBasketballPlayer : Person → Prop
axiom isAmerican : Person → Prop
axiom canDoJumpShots : Person → Prop

theorem yuri_not_american_player : ¬ (isProfessionalBasketballPlayer yuri ∧ isAmerican yuri) := by
```

4. `jump_shots_leap` shows Yuri must leap straight into air
5. `leap_activates_muscles` shows this activates leg muscles
6. `yuri_no_activate` contradicts the activation result

The existence of non-American players remains unused, as it doesn't affect Yuri's individual case. The contradiction stems directly from the physical requirements of basketball and Yuri's physiological constraints.
