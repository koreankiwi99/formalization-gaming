The premises state that Susan flies to LGA (from some departure) and John flies from LGA (to some arrival), with the constraint that departure and arrival airports must differ for any flight. However, there is no premise connecting Susan's flight to John's, nor any requirement that John must have a flight arriving at LGA. John's flight from LGA must arrive at a different airport (by axiom P2), but this does not imply he has another flight to LGA. The premises do not prohibit John from having additional flights, but they also do not provide evidence that he flies to LGA. Therefore, the conclusion is not entailed by the premises.
