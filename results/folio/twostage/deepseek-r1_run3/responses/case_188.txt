The premises establish a chain of implications from being a professional basketball player to activating leg muscles. Since Yuri does not activate his leg muscles, he cannot be a professional basketball player. Therefore, he cannot be both American and a professional basketball player.

Here is the complete Lean code with the proof:

<lean>
axiom obj : Type
axiom Yuri : obj
axiom PB : obj → Prop  -- Professional basketball player
axiom American : obj → Prop  -- American national
axiom JumpShots : obj → Prop  -- Can do jump shots
axiom LeapAir : obj → Prop  -- Leaps straight into the air
axiom ActivateLegs : obj → Prop  -- Activates leg muscles

-- Conclusion: Yuri is not an American professional basketball player
theorem goal : ¬ (American Yuri ∧ PB Yuri) := 
  fun h => 
    have pb_yuri : PB Yuri := h.right
    have jump_yuri : JumpShots Yuri := all_pb_jump Yuri pb_yuri
    have leap_yuri : LeapAir Yuri := jump_to_leap Yuri jump_yuri
