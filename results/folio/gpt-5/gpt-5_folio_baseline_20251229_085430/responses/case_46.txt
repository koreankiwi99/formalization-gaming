<lean>
axiom Person : Type
axiom Season : Type

axiom Spring : Season
axiom Summer : Season
axiom Fall : Season
axiom Winter : Season

axiom Emma : Person
axiom Mia : Person
axiom James : Person

axiom Student : Person → Prop
axiom WantsLongVacation : Person → Prop
axiom FavoriteSeason : Person → Season → Prop

-- All students who want a long vacation have summer as their favorite season
axiom R1 : ∀ x : Person, Student x → WantsLongVacation x → FavoriteSeason x Summer

-- Emma's favorite season is summer
axiom T1 : FavoriteSeason Emma Summer

-- Mia's favorite season is not the same as Emma's (since Emma's is summer)
axiom T2 : ¬ FavoriteSeason Mia Summer

-- James wants to have a long vacation
axiom T3 : WantsLongVacation James

-- We cannot derive FavoriteSeason James Summer without knowing that James is a student,
-- and we also cannot derive its negation from the given axioms.
-- Hence, the truth of "James's favorite season is summer" is uncertain.
</lean>

ANSWER: Uncertain
