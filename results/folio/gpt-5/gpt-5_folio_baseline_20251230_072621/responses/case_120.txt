<lean>
axiom obj : Type
axiom Affection : obj → Prop
axiom Love : obj → Prop
axiom Positive : obj → Prop

-- Some affection is love.
axiom E1 : ∃ x : obj, Affection x ∧ Love x
-- Some love is positive.
axiom E2 : ∃ x : obj, Love x ∧ Positive x

-- From E1 and E2, we cannot derive ∃ x, Affection x ∧ Positive x in general,
-- nor can we derive its negation from the given information.
-- Therefore, the truth of "Some affection is positive" is uncertain.
</lean>

ANSWER: Uncertain
