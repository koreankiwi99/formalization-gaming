-- The Harding Pegmatite Mine, located in the Picuris Mountains, was donated.

axiom T_harding_loc_picuris : LocatedIn HardingPegmatiteMine Picuris
axiom T_harding_donated : Donated HardingPegmatiteMine

-- There are no mountain ranges in Texas that have mines that have been donated.
axiom R_no_tx_mr_with_donated_mine :
  ∀ r m, MountainRange r → InTexas r → Mine m → LocatedIn m r → ¬Donated m

-- From the above, Picuris cannot be in Texas (since it has a donated mine located in it).

  intro hTX

  exact hnotdon T_harding_donated
