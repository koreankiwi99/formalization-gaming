   - If Taylor visited the gym daily, she would fulfill nutritional intakes, implying she grew up health-conscious—contradicting the second case.
   - Thus, Taylor does not visit the gym daily.

4. **Lean Proof**:

   - Case analysis on Taylor's disjunction shows the first case leads to contradiction.
   - The second case directly provides ¬GrewUpHealthConsciousHome Taylor.
   - Assuming VisitsGymDaily Taylor leads to contradiction via the implication chain.
   - Therefore, ¬VisitsGymDaily Taylor is proven.
