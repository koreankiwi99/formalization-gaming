Additionally, gaining knowledge causes a person to become smarter. Therefore, Harry gained knowledge and became smarter. This proves the existence of at least one person (Harry) who is smarter and has gained knowledge. Thus, the statement "A smarter person has gained knowledge" is true, as there exists such a person.

<lean>
axiom Person : Type
axiom Book : Type
axiom walden : Book
axiom harry : Person
axiom Read : Person → Book → Prop
axiom GainKnowledge : Person → Prop
axiom Smarter : Person → Prop
axiom reading_knowledge : ∀ p b, Read p b → GainKnowledge p
axiom knowledge_smarter : ∀ p, GainKnowledge p → Smarter p
axiom harry_read_walden : Read harry walden

theorem smarter_exists_gained : ∃ p, Smarter p ∧ GainKnowledge p :=
  ⟨harry, 
   ⟨knowledge_smarter harry (reading_knowledge harry walden harry_read_walden), 
    reading_knowledge harry walden harry_read_walden⟩⟩
</lean>
