The statement is: "Imperium doesn't have a feud with a professional wrestling stable that includes Ivy Nile." This means that for all stables, if the stable includes Ivy Nile, then Imperium does not feud with it. However, the context states that Diamond Mine includes Ivy Nile and Imperium has a feud with Diamond Mine. Therefore, Diamond Mine serves as a counterexample: it is a stable that includes Ivy Nile and has a feud with Imperium. This directly contradicts the universal statement.

Thus, the statement is false.

theorem statement_false : ¬ (∀ s, Member IvyNile s → ¬ Feud Imperium s) :=
