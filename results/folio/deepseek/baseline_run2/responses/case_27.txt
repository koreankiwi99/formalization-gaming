     - Thus, this case is impossible.

     - `¬FromEarth Marvin` holds, so `Extraterrestrial Marvin` (by axiom 7).
     - Thus, `¬Human Marvin` (by axiom 3).
     - However, we cannot prove `Alien Marvin`:
       - Axiom 2 only states that being from Mars implies being an alien, not vice versa.
       - There is no axiom forcing an extraterrestrial (like Marvin) to be an alien.
     - Similarly, we cannot prove `¬Alien Marvin` because there might be aliens not from Mars.
