   - Apply the first premise to `a`: since `a` was bred back, it must be artificially selected.
   - Thus, `a` is a Heck cattle that is artificially selected, proving `∃ a, HeckCattle a ∧ ArtificiallySelected a`.

3. **Lean Code**:
   - The proof uses existential elimination (`rcases`) and implication application.

-- Premise: Heck cattle were bred back (implies existence)

theorem some_heck_selected : ∃ a : Animal, HeckCattle a ∧ ArtificiallySelected a := by

  have h_art : ArtificiallySelected a := breeding_back_is_artificial a h_bred
  exact ⟨a, h_heck, h_art⟩
