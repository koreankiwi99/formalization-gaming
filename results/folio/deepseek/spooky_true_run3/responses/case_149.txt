- All books published by New Vessel Press are in English (premise 2).
- Neapolitan Chronicles is published by New Vessel Press (premise 3).
- Therefore, Neapolitan Chronicles must be an English book.

The additional information about translation specialization and Palace of Flies is irrelevant to this conclusion. The premises directly support the statement.

<lean>
axiom Book : Type
axiom IsPublishedByNVP : Book → Prop
axiom IsEnglishBook : Book → Prop
axiom neapolitanChronicles : Book
axiom all_nvp_books_english : ∀ b : Book, IsPublishedByNVP b → IsEnglishBook b
axiom nc_published_by_nvp : IsPublishedByNVP neapolitanChronicles
theorem nc_english : IsEnglishBook neapolitanChronicles :=
  all_nvp_books_english neapolitanChronicles nc_published_by_nvp
