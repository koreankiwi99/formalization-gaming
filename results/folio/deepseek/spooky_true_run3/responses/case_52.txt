German is related to Spanish (a Romance language), but this does not necessitate German being Romance, as external languages can relate to family members without belonging to the family. Basque's isolation further demonstrates that unrelatedness doesn't imply non-Romance status. No premise forces a language related to a Romance language to be Romance itself. Thus, the conclusion cannot be proven.
