The reasoning involves proving that Ailton Silva was loaned out to a football club based on the given premises. Premise 2 states that Ailton (identified as Ailton Silva) was loaned out to Braga. Premise 4 establishes Braga as a football club. Therefore, Braga serves as a witness satisfying the existential claim that Ailton Silva was loaned out to a football club.

<lean>
axiom obj : Type
axiom AiltonSilva : obj
axiom Braga : obj
axiom Nautico : obj
axiom Fluminense : obj
axiom IsFootballClub : obj → Prop
axiom LoanedOutTo : obj → obj → Prop

-- Premise 2: Ailton (Silva) was loaned to Braga
axiom loaned_to_Braga : LoanedOutTo AiltonSilva Braga

-- Premise 4: Braga is a football club (along with Nautico)
axiom Braga_club : IsFootballClub Braga

-- Prove Ailton Silva was loaned to a football club
theorem loaned_to_club : ∃ club, IsFootballClub club ∧ LoanedOutTo AiltonSilva club :=
  by
    apply Exists.intro Braga
    exact ⟨Braga_club, loaned_to_Braga⟩
</lean>

ANSWER: True
