The premises establish that Harry is a bee and bees do not reproduce by mating. Vertebrates have backbones and reproduce by mating, so bees cannot be vertebrates and must be invertebrates. However, there is no premise linking bees to queen bees beyond the fact that queen bees are a subset of bees. Knowing Harry is a bee does not imply Harry is a queen bee, as there could be non-queen bees. Thus, the conclusion cannot be proven from the premises.
