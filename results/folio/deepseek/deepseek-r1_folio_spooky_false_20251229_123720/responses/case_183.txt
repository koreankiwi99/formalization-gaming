The statement to evaluate is a conditional: "If Tom is not both grumpy and mean to animals, then Tom is neither grumpy nor an animal lover." However, since Tom is an animal lover, the consequent "Tom is neither grumpy nor an animal lover" is false. The antecedent is true because Tom is not mean to animals. Therefore, the entire conditional is false, as a true antecedent cannot imply a false consequent.

theorem conditional_false :
