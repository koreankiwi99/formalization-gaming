The statement "The security deposit at the Olive Garden is either $2000 or more" directly follows from the premises. The general rule states that in any managed building, the security deposit equals or exceeds the monthly rent. Since the Olive Garden is a managed building with a monthly rent of $2000, its security deposit must be at least $2000. The Lean proof formalizes this without relying on unrelated premises about pets or Tom.

<lean>
axiom ManagedBuilding : Type
axiom OliveGarden : ManagedBuilding
axiom monthlyRent : ManagedBuilding → Nat
axiom securityDeposit : ManagedBuilding → Nat

axiom deposit_rule : ∀ b : ManagedBuilding, 
  securityDeposit b ≥ monthlyRent b

axiom olive_rent : monthlyRent OliveGarden = 2000

theorem olive_deposit : 
  securityDeposit OliveGarden ≥ 2000 := by
  rw [← olive_rent]
  exact deposit_rule OliveGarden
</lean>

ANSWER: True
