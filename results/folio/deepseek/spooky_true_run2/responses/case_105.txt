The statement "It costs below US $300 to take the GRE test" directly follows from the premise "It costs $205 to take the GRE test, which is cheaper than $300." Since 205 is less than 300, the conclusion is provable without needing the other contextual details about financial aid or Tom's situation.

<lean>
axiom gre_cost : Nat
axiom cost_fact : gre_cost = 205

theorem cost_below_300 : gre_cost < 300 := by
  rw [cost_fact]
  decide
</lean>

ANSWER: True
