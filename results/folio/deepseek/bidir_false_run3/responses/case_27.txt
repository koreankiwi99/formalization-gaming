Specifically, Marvin being from both Earth and Mars is impossible due to conflicting human/non-human requirements, forcing the "neither Earth nor Mars" scenario. Here, Marvin is an extraterrestrial (per T3) but has no Mars origin (so A2 doesn't apply). Since no premises constrain extraterrestrials to necessarily be aliens, Marvin could be an alien or not. Thus proving "¬Alien Marvin" is impossible.
