The premises and the statement "Marvin is neither a human nor from Mars" are consistent. After formalizing the reasoning in Lean, an attempt to prove the negation of the statement (i.e., that Marvin is either human or from Mars) fails in the case where Marvin is not from Earth and not from Mars. In this case, Marvin is extraterrestrial (by A7) and thus not human (by A3), and not from Mars—which directly supports the original statement. A contradiction is only found if Marvin is from both Earth and Mars, but that scenario is eliminated by the disjunction in A6 and leads to a separate contradiction not affecting the proof of the statement's falsity. Thus, the statement cannot be proven false.
