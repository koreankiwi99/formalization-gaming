The reasoning is straightforward based on the premises. 
- The first premise states that all Istanbul plates begin with 34 (universal quantification). 
- The third premise states Joe's plate is from Istanbul. 
- Therefore, Joe's plate must begin with 34. 

The information about Tom's plate (beginning with 35) and the rule that 35 precludes 34 is irrelevant to Joe's plate. 

Here's the Lean proof:

<lean>
axiom Plate : Type
axiom joePlate : Plate
axiom Istanbul : Plate → Prop
axiom BeginsWith34 : Plate → Prop
axiom T1 : ∀ p : Plate, Istanbul p → BeginsWith34 p
axiom T2 : Istanbul joePlate
theorem joeBeginsWith34 : BeginsWith34 joePlate := T1 joePlate T2
</lean>

ANSWER: True
