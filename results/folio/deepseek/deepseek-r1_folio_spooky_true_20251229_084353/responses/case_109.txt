axiom P1 : ∀ p : Person, SpillsFood p → ¬ Tidy p
axiom P2 : ∀ p : Person, ClumsyFoodie p → SpillsFood p
axiom P3 : ∀ p : Person, Cleanly p → Tidy p
axiom P4 : ∀ p : Person, ValuesOrder p → Cleanly p
axiom P5 : ∀ p : Person, FamilyPrioritizes p → ValuesOrder p

- Not being cleanly implies not having a family that prioritizes order (via the chain: family prioritizes → values order → cleanly).

Therefore, Peter is neither a clumsy foodie nor has a family that prioritizes order, proving the conclusion.
