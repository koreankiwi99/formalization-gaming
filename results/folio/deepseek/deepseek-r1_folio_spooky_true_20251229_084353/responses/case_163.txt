The proof attempts to show that Breaking Bad airs at 8 pm Monday. However, while the premises establish that:
1. There exists *some* show airing at 8 pm Monday that gives roses
2. That show must be The Bachelor (reality TV)
3. Breaking Bad is not reality TV (so it's not the same show)

The premises don't connect Breaking Bad to Monday 8 pm. They don't state:
- That *only* one show airs at that time
- That Breaking Bad airs at a different time
- Any relationship between non-reality shows and time slots

Thus, we cannot prove the claim from the given premises.
