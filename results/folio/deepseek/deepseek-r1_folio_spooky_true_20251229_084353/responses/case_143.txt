- Heck cattle were bred back to resemble aurochs (implying Heck cattle resemble aurochs).
- Heck cattle are animals that underwent breeding back.
- Some animals that were bred back resemble extinct animals (existential: there exists at least one such animal and extinct animal).

However, the existential premise does not specify that Heck cattle are the animals resembling an extinct animal. Even if Heck cattle resemble aurochs, they might resemble another extinct animal (not necessarily aurochs). Resemblance is not unique or transitive in a way that forces aurochs to be extinct. Thus, the conclusion cannot be proven from the premises.
