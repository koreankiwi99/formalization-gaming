This implies Marvin is an extraterrestrial (by premise 7) and highly intelligent (premise 5). However, there is no premise stating that all extraterrestrials are aliens; only that aliens are extraterrestrials (premise 1) and Martians are aliens (premise 2). Since Marvin is not from Mars, and no other premise forces him to be an alien, it is impossible to prove that Marvin is not an alien. Thus, the negation of "Marvin is an alien" cannot be proven from the premises.
