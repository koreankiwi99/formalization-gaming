The reasoning is valid based on the premises. The key steps are:
1. "If a game sells more than one million copies, then it will be included in the Top 10 list" (universal rule)
2. "The Legend of Zelda sold more than one million copies" (specific fact)
3. Therefore, The Legend of Zelda must be on the Top 10 list (modus ponens)

The premise about Japanese companies is not needed for this conclusion.

<lean>
axiom Game : Type
axiom zelda : Game
axiom Top10 : Game → Prop
axiom SellsOver1M : Game → Prop
axiom sells_z : SellsOver1M zelda
axiom sales_rule : ∀ g : Game, SellsOver1M g → Top10 g
theorem zelda_in_top10 : Top10 zelda := sales_rule zelda sells_z
</lean>

ANSWER: True
