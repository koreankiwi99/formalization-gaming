1. All Brown Swiss cattle are cows.
2. Some pets are Brown Swiss cattle.
3. All cows are domesticated animals.
4. Alligators are not domesticated animals.
5. Ted is an alligator.

To prove: "Ted is a pet and Brown Swiss cattle".

Analysis:
- Ted is an alligator (axiom 5).
- Alligators are not domesticated animals (axiom 4), so Ted cannot be a domesticated animal.
- If Ted were Brown Swiss cattle, then by axiom 1, Ted would be a cow. By axiom 3, Ted would be a domesticated animal. This contradicts axiom 4.
- Thus, Ted cannot be Brown Swiss cattle.
- The statement requires Ted to be both a pet and Brown Swiss cattle, but Ted cannot be Brown Swiss cattle.
- While some pets are Brown Swiss cattle (axiom 2), there is no evidence that Ted specifically is a pet.

Conclusion: The statement cannot be proven from the premises.
