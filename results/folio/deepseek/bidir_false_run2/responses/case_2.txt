The goal is to prove this statement false by showing its negation under the given context.

The negation of the statement is: Bonnie either chaperones or performs often, but she is not both an aspiring academic and an inactive member. This follows from:

- **Second subgoal**: Show ¬(Y ∧ I) for Bonnie. Assume Y and I hold. Then ax5 gives S Bonnie, while ax4 and ax3 give ¬S Bonnie, a contradiction. Thus, Y and I are contradictory.
