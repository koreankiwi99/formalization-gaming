The premises indicate that Mike is an international student who must apply for CPT if he wants to work. However, the requirement to apply for CPT/OPT when wanting work is only specified for F1 visa holders. Nothing is stated about requirements for J1 visa holders. Since Mike's visa type (F1 or J1) is unknown and both remain consistent with the given premises, we cannot derive a contradiction from assuming Mike has a J1 visa.
