The context provides:

- Symphony No. 9 is a music piece.
- Vienna Music Society (an orchestra) premiered Symphony No. 9.

This directly implies that there exists at least one orchestra (Vienna Music Society) that premiered at least one music piece (Symphony No. 9). No additional reasoning about composers, conductors, or leadership is required to establish this existential claim.

<lean>
axiom entity : Type
axiom Symphony9 : entity
axiom ViennaMusicSociety : entity

axiom MusicPiece : entity → Prop
axiom Orchestra : entity → Prop
axiom Premiered : entity → entity → Prop

axiom ax1 : MusicPiece Symphony9
axiom ax2 : Orchestra ViennaMusicSociety
axiom ax3 : Premiered ViennaMusicSociety Symphony9

theorem some_orchestra_premiered :
  ∃ (o m : entity), Orchestra o ∧ MusicPiece m ∧ Premiered o m :=
by
  apply Exists.intro ViennaMusicSociety
  apply Exists.intro Symphony9
  exact ⟨ax2, ax1, ax3⟩

axiom ax4 : Wrote Beethoven Symphony9
