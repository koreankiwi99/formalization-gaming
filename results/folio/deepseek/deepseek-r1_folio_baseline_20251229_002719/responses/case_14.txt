The context states that the Legend of Zelda was created by a Japanese game company, all games on the Top 10 list are made by Japanese companies, and any game selling over one million copies is included in the Top 10 list. Since the Legend of Zelda sold over one million copies, it must be on the Top 10 list. Therefore, the statement "The Legend of Zelda is not on the Top 10 list" is false.

<lean>
axiom obj : Type
axiom Legend_of_Zelda : obj
axiom JapaneseGameCompanyMade : obj → Prop
axiom OnTop10List : obj → Prop
axiom SellsOverOneMillion : obj → Prop

-- A Japanese game company created The Legend of Zelda
axiom T1 : JapaneseGameCompanyMade Legend_of_Zelda

-- All games on the Top 10 list are made by Japanese game companies
axiom R1 : ∀ g : obj, OnTop10List g → JapaneseGameCompanyMade g

-- Games selling over one million copies are on the Top 10 list
axiom R2 : ∀ g : obj, SellsOverOneMillion g → OnTop10List g

-- The Legend of Zelda sold over one million copies
axiom T2 : SellsOverOneMillion Legend_of_Zelda

-- Proof that Legend_of_Zelda is on the Top 10 list
theorem zelda_on_top_10 : OnTop10List Legend_of_Zelda := 
  R2 Legend_of_Zelda T2

-- Since we proved OnTop10List Legend_of_Zelda, the negation is false
theorem zelda_not_off_list : ¬¬OnTop10List Legend_of_Zelda := 
  fun h => h zelda_on_top_10
</lean>

ANSWER: False
