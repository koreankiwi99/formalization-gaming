Specifically, the last premise states that Hannah works in student jobs on campus, and if she needs to earn money for tuition, then she is not picky and does not need to earn money. Thus, the conclusion that Hannah needs to earn money is false.

theorem not_need_money : ¬NeedMoney Hannah := by
