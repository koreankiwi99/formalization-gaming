The reasoning text involves seasons and students' preferences. The goal is to determine if we can prove that "Mia's favorite season is spring" is false based on the given premises.

**Premises:**
1. Four distinct seasons: Spring, Summer, Fall, Winter.
2. For any student wanting a long vacation, their favorite season is summer.
3. Emma's favorite season is summer.
4. Mia's favorite season differs from Emma's.
5. James wants a long vacation.

**Analysis:**
- From premise 3 (`EmmaFav Summer`) and premise 4 (`MiaFav ≠ EmmaFav`), **Mia's favorite season cannot be summer**.
- Premise 2 (`∀ s, WantsLongVacation s → Favorites s Summer`) shows James must have summer as his favorite, but provides no direct constraint on Mia because Mia's vacation preferences are unspecified.
- We know Mia's favorite is **not summer**, but it could be **Spring, Fall, or Winter**. No premises:
  - Forbid Mia from liking spring.
  - Require Mia to have specific seasons besides summer.
- Thus, we **cannot prove** that Mia's favorite season isn't spring.

**Conclusion:**  
The negation "¬(Mia's favorite season is spring)" cannot be proven from the premises.

<lean>
axiom Student : Type
axiom Season : Type
axiom Spring : Season
axiom Summer : Season
axiom Fall : Season
axiom Winter : Season
axiom season_distinct : Spring ≠ Summer ∧ Spring ≠ Fall ∧ Spring ≠ Winter ∧ Summer ≠ Fall ∧ Summer ≠ Winter ∧ Fall ≠ Winter

-- FavoriteSeason maps a student to their favorite season
axiom Favorites : Student → Season
axiom WantsLongVacation : Student → Prop

-- Given students
axiom Emma : Student
axiom Mia : Student
axiom James : Student

-- Premises
axiom emma_fav_summer : Favorites Emma = Summer
axiom mia_not_emma : Favorites Mia ≠ Favorites Emma
axiom long_vacation_fav : ∀ s : Student, WantsLongVacation s → Favorites s = Summer
axiom james_wants : WantsLongVacation James

-- Mia's favorite is not summer (derived)
theorem mia_not_summer : Favorites Mia ≠ Summer := by
  intro h
  apply mia_not_emma
  rw [emma_fav_summer, h]

-- Cannot prove Mia's favorite isn't Spring (no constraint forces her season choice)
</lean>

ANSWER: Failure
