2. Pets are either monkeys or birds (A3)

4. Animals that are pets breathe (A5)
5. Birds that are pets can fly (A4)
6. Pets that can fly have wings (A6)
7. Additionally, we know that Rock either flies, is a bird, or cannot breathe (A8)

The proof proceeds by cases:
- If Rock can fly (A8 case 1), we directly use A6 to get wings
- If Rock is a bird (A8 case 2), we show it can fly (A4) then get wings (A6)
- In the last case (Rock cannot breathe), we show:
  - Rock must be a bird (monkey would imply mammal→animal→breathe, contradicting ¬breathe)
  - Then proceed as in the bird case
