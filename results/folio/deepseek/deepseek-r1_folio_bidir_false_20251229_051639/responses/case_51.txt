1. If Basque were a Romance language, then by the language family axiom, Basque must be related to every other Romance language (such as Spanish and French).
2. However, the premise states that Basque is not related to any other language (where "other" implies distinctness).
3. Using the distinctness of Basque from Spanish and French (inherent in named entities), we derive a contradiction: Basque is both related and not related to Spanish (or French).

Thus, we prove that Basque cannot be a Romance language.
